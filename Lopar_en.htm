Position of the Lopar

Lopar is situated on the island Rab, in the Kvarner group of islands. It is 110 km from the administrative center of Rijeka, 190 km from Zagreb.

Lopar is beautiful tourist place 14 km away from the old town of Rab.

The place can be reached by ferry Jablanac - Mišnjak, where the ride takes about fifteen minutes or ferry to the island Krk from Valbiska. This line is more interesting to the guests because of proximity to Rijeka and airport.

Beaches

Lopar abound with many beautiful sand beaches. There are even 22, of which 3 are nudist (Ciganka, Sahara i Stolac).

Lopar's largest and the most popular beach is Rajska plaža (Paradise Beach), 1.5 km long, along which are located the most important tourist facilities, and which have since 2003. a Blue Flag, an international symbol of quality and preservation of the environment and the safety of swimmers.

History

The most famous historical figure is certainly Lopare stonemason St. Marin born in the 4th century. He is considered to be the founder of the Republic of San Marino.
