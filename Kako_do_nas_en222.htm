How to get to Rab

From Italy: follow the signs to Rijeka (Fiume), from Rijeka follow signs to Senj and Split.

From Austria (via Ljubljana): follow signs to Postojna, then the Illirska Bistrica and Rijeka, and from Rijeka towards Senj and Split.

From Hungary (via Zagreb): take A1 highway towards Split and exit Žuta Lokva, and then drive towards Senj and coastal road to Stinica.

If you arrive by plane, the nearest airport is on the island Krk, and if you arrive by train, the nearest railway station is in Rijeka.

Ferries

The island can be reached from the south side (if coming from the mainland) using ferry line Stinica - Mišnjak.

Ferries sail between Rab Stinica - Mišnjak 8 - 20 times a day (more frequently in the season) in both directions.
The ride takes about 20 min.











If you are coming to the island from the north side (from the island of Krk) use the ferry Valbiska - Lopar.

SplitTours ferries on line Valbiska (on the island Krk) - Lopar (in Rab) sail 4 times a day.

The ride takes about 90 min.

How to get to Lopar

If arriving in Lopar using ferry line Valbiska (Krk) - Lopar (Rab) get off from the ferry, and you are already in the "old" Lopar. Drive only two to three minute along the main road and you'll come to a crossroads. Turn right to Supetarska Drago, Mundanije, Barbat, Rab, Barbat and Mišnjak, and continue driving in the same direction. You'll enter the "new" Lopar where the situated hotel complex San Marino , titled Auto-camp, a multitude of sports facilities, theme parks and famous Rajska plaža - Paradise Beach.

If arriving by ferry from the Stinica to Mišnjak, twenty minutes drive across the island through Barbat, Barbat, Mundanije and Supetarska Draga will get you to Lopar.

How to get to the Apartment

When you come by main road from the direction of Rab and the Supetar Drage and enter Lopar at the crossroad turn right towards the hotel resort and camp, "San Marino".

After 150 meters (after the bakery), turn left at the next intersection, and after 50 meters, turn right. At house number 397 on the right side you will see the apartment, "Ivan".

Only ten minutes of pleasant walk to 400 meters distant famous "Paradise beach", and proximity to the restaurant (50 meters), bakeries, pharmacies, shops (all at a distance of 150 - 200 meters) make this apartment so close and yet so far away from noise and crowd.
