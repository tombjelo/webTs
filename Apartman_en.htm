Apartment position

When you reach Lopar from the main road from the direction of Rab, Mundanije and Supetarska Drage , turn right towards the hotel resort and camping, "San Marino".

After 150 meters (after the bakery), turn left at the next intersection, and after 50 meters, turn right. At house number 397 on the right side you will see an Apartment "Ivan".

Precisely this kind of accommodation in the "other" street ensures peace and quiet which most of the guests want.

Only ten minutes of pleasant walk to 400 meters distant famous "Paradise beach", and proximity to the restaurant (50 meters), bakeries, pharmacies, shops (all at a distance of 150 - 200 meters) make this apartment so close and yet so far away from noise and crowd.

The apartment is designed to accommodate 4-5 people, a categorization of the three stars are a guarantee of quality accommodation and a large number of additional facilities in and around the apartment.

The house is surrounded by flowers and greenery, so it gives the impression discreetly hidden ideal place for a relaxing holiday.

Fig, olive, palm, laurel, lavender, rosemary and several kinds of flowers contributes to the look of the Mediterranean atmosphere of accommodation and provide the guests not only the relaxation for body but also for the eyes

At the beginning of the courtyard, shaded by vines, next to the terrace your car is protected by sun.

Terrace

The apartment has a dozen square terrace that is protected by the shade of vines, beneath which is a table with six chairs.

Large detached stone grill with running water is available to you 0-24 hours.

Living room and kitchen

Living room and kitchen are the biggest area of our apartment in which you spend most of the time. This area has a TV with satellite antenna, air conditioning, and a large sofa that can be extended.

Kitchen part is separated by bar that is also a desk, has all the necessary elements you need: gas stove with oven, sink with hot water, refrigerator, cupboards with dishes, a large table with corner bench and chairs.

Bedroom

The bedroom is located on the east (colder) part of the house, and you will find there peace and a comfortable temperature in the afternoon and evening hours.

The room has two beds (double bed), with its closets connected by a bridge and small bedside tables.

Children's Room

Children's room has two large beds in which can also sleep adults. In the room, next to the bed, are two cupboards and a wardrobe.

Hallway - Bathroom - Toilet

The first room that you enter is the hallway with wardrobe and cabinet for shoes. From there, one enters the living room, bedroom, bathroom and separate toilet.

The bathroom has a washing machine that is always at your disposal.
